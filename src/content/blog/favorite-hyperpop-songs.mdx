## Hyperpop - the genre

Hyperpop is a genre of music that emerged in the late 2010s, early 2020s. It's a bit hard to categorize, but I would say most songs are a blend of rap, pop, and electronic music, with liberal use of autotune and distortion.

I was first introduced to the genre sometime around ~2022, and I explored the genre through obscure playlists recommended to me through the youtube algorithm. Many of these songs have only a few thousand plays on streaming platforms.
